Our story

Your Trusted Dallas HVAC Partner

To deliver honest, high-quality HVAC services that keep Dallas homes comfortable and safe, with a focus on customer satisfaction and community values.

Regal HVAC Contractors was founded in 2010 by a team of experienced technicians who saw a need for reliable, customer-focused HVAC service in Dallas. Starting with just one truck, we built a reputation for showing up on time, diagnosing problems accurately, and fixing them permanently. Our commitment to transparency and fair pricing quickly earned us a loyal customer base across the city.

Today, we serve thousands of homeowners and businesses throughout Dallas, from Lakewood to Preston Hollow. Our team has grown, but our core values remain the same: treat every home like our own, communicate clearly, and never cut corners. We're proud to be a locally owned company that puts people before profits.
